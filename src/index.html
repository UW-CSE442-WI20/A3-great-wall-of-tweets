Great Wall of Trump Tweets

This site allows you to visualize and observe what Donald Trump tweeted in 2015-16 leading up to the presidential election. The scatter plot charts tweets by date and popularity. Popularity value is a normalized log of the sum of retweets and likes.

Explore the scatter plot on your own utilizing these functions:

  • Tooltips show the tweet's text of the pointed sample
  • Use search to filter tweets by keywords and phrases
  • Zoom in and out for more detail
